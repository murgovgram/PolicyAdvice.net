Gold and silver jewelry and precious stones are valuable assets, which is why insurance for jewelry exists in the first place. But not all insurance companies are the same. Since jewelry is a significant investment, it pays to get the best jewelry insurance you can find. And that’s what we are going to talk about in this review.

Methodology

You already know about homeowners insurance, pets insurance, and even individual insurance. But what about getting a jewelry insurance policy? Well, if you have been wondering about the same thing, then you’re at the right place.

We selected the best jewelry insurance companies by researching multiple insurance policies, then picking the top contenders.

We chose the best providers based on: The type of coverage offered The policy terms The customer satisfaction rating The company’s reputation (AM Best, BBB) We’ve also broken down all of this information into digestible, bite-size pieces so that it’s easily understandable even by people who are new to the jewelry insurance market.

Individual Companies Reviews

If you are into purchasing jewelry from Etsy and other online marketplaces or haven’t moved past the costume jewelry phase, then you probably haven’t thought about getting insurance on jewelry, and you don’t need to.

However, the situation is different if you tend to purchase your jewelry from large brands and fashion stores that feature precious stones and metals and come with a fancy price tag. In this case, you absolutely need to find a reliable jewelry insurance company.

Similar to other types of insurance, there are many options to choose from when it comes to getting your jewelry insurance. Along with basic personal jewelry insurance, there are also homeowners and renters insurance offering jewelry coverage.

How Much Does Jewelry Insurance Cost?

Now that we’ve got a good idea of what jewelry insurance is and what it covers, it’s time to move deeper into the importance of jewelry insurance. Let’s answer the most frequently asked question by those looking for reliable jewelry insurance, which is, “How much is jewelry insurance?”

Contrary to popular belief, jewelry insurance does not have to cost you an arm and a leg. While many folks think that jewelry insurance means having to pay high premiums and other hidden charges because of the expensive nature of the items being insured, in reality, it’s quite the opposite.

If you’d like an answer to the question, “How much does jewelry insurance cost?” then you’ll be surprised to know that a typical jewelry insurance policy is only going to cost you 1% to 2% of the total value of the jewelry being insured. That’s an incredibly reasonable price considering the solid coverage that most jewelry insurance providers have to offer.

With jewelry insurance prices so low, getting a policy is a no-brainer if you want to protect your jewelry from unforeseen circumstances. That being said, the total cost of the jewelry insurance policy also depends on a few factors such as:

The worth of the jewelry The amount of insured jewelry The condition of the jewelry at the time of getting a policy quote Since jewelry can be easily stolen, to keep jewelry insurance coverage as affordable as possible, the standard policy liability limit for robbery is around $1,500. It means that the insurance provider will not pay more than the amount specified in the insurance agreement, which in this case is $1,500. However, the amount may vary depending on your circumstances and the particular insurance provider.
